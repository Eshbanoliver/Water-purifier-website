About Shantiraj Associates

Your Trusted Kent RO
Water Purifier Partner

Shantiraj Associates has been serving the people of Jaipur for over 15 years as an authorized Kent RO dealer and water purifier specialist. We offer the complete range of Kent water purifiers, including the Kent Pearl, Kent Prime, Kent Superb, Kent Supreme, and Kent Ace — each backed by genuine manufacturer warranty and professional support.

Our Mission: To ensure every family in Jaipur has access to safe, pure, and affordable drinking water. We believe clean water is a fundamental right, not a luxury — and we're committed to making advanced Kent RO technology accessible to every household and business in Rajasthan.

  • Same-day installation and setup by certified technicians
  • Comprehensive AMC plans with scheduled maintenance visits
  • 2000+ happy families served across Jaipur, Sanganer & Pratap Nagar
